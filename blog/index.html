My secrect recipe gourmet chilly oil


Ingredients part A:

  • 1/2 ginger sliced
  • 2 green onion whole
  • 1/2 onion sliced
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cardamom (crush it and take the seeds out) (optional)

Ingredients part B:

  • 2 cups of oil
  • 1/3 cup chilly powder (for the color)
  • 1/3 cup chilly flakes (for the texture)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of five spice powder
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame
  • 1 tablespoon of black vinegar

SOP

  1. Add oil to pot, and heat it to 460 F degree (leave at least half of the pot room for frying, fry ingredients and for black vinegar “boils”)
  2. Let oil temperature cool down to 300 F degree
  3. Add part A ingredients into oil let it fry until onion and ginger are golden brown
  4. Take all part A ingredients out of pot (just need part A ingredients for flavor)
  5. Heat the oil temperature to 400 F degree (this step is important, if didn’t reach 400 degree, it will have the raw taste from onion and ginger in the chilly oil)
  6. Let oil cool down to 360 F degree
  7. Add sesame, let it fry for 1 minute
  8. Add chilly powder and chilly flakes, stir mix
  9. Add black vinegar (there will be strong bubbles so be careful) (this step is to cool down the oil temperature to avoid chilly powder burnt, and also to add more flavor. Black vinegar will evaporate and the chilly oil will not taste sour at all)
